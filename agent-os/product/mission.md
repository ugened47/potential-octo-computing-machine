# Product Mission

## Pitch

**AI Video Clipper** transforms hours of manual video editing into minutes of AI-powered automation. We automatically transcribe videos, remove silence, find highlights, and generate clips ready for social media.

**From 3 hours of manual editing to 10 minutes of review.**

## Problem Statement

Content creators spend **3-5 hours editing each video**, with 80% of that time spent on repetitive tasks:
- Removing silent pauses
- Finding key moments
- Creating highlights for social media
- Adding subtitles/transcriptions

## Solution

AI Video Clipper is a web application that automatically:
- Transcribes video with timestamps
- Removes silence and pauses
- Finds highlights based on keywords
- Generates clips ready for export

## Target Users

### Primary
- YouTube creators (1K-100K subscribers)
- Podcasters
- Educational content creators
- Live streamers

### Secondary
- Marketing teams
- Course creators
- Social media managers

## Unique Value Proposition

"Professional video editing automation at $19/month - 10x faster than manual editing"

### Key Differentiators
1. **Speed**: Process 30-min video in <5 minutes
2. **Price**: $19/month vs competitors' $24-40
3. **Simplicity**: 5-minute onboarding, intuitive UI
4. **Focus**: Specialized in highlights, not a bloated all-in-one tool

## Core Features (MVP)

1. **User Authentication** - Email/password + Google OAuth
2. **Video Upload** - Drag-and-drop, direct to S3
3. **Automatic Transcription** - Speech-to-text with timestamps
4. **Silence Removal** - Automatic detection and removal
5. **Keyword Search & Clipping** - Find and create clips from keywords
6. **Timeline Editor** - Visual timeline with waveform and transcript sync
7. **Video Export** - Multiple resolutions (720p, 1080p)
8. **Dashboard** - Manage all uploaded videos

## Business Model

### Pricing Tiers
- **Free**: $0/month - 3 videos, 30 minutes, 1 GB storage, 720p export
- **Creator**: $19/month - 20 videos, 150 minutes, 10 GB, 1080p, priority processing
- **Pro**: $39/month - 50 videos, 400 minutes, 50 GB, API access, batch processing

## Vision

To become the go-to platform for content creators who want professional-quality video editing automation without the complexity and cost of traditional tools. We focus on doing one thing exceptionally well: making video highlights effortless.
